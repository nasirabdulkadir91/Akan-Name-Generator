GHANA AKAN NAMING

Ghana is a country located along the Gulf of Guinea and Atlantic Ocean in West Africa.

FUN FACT

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.

Day Boys Girls
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abena
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yao Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Amma
